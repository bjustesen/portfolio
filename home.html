About Me

I am Brooke Justesen and am a Web Design and Development major. I currently live in Rexburg Idaho and have been married to my husband, Tyler for 3 years and recently welcomed a son into our family.

I became interested in web design after tutoring for the WDD 130, Web Fundamentals, course at Brigham Young University-Idaho. I was able to have an internship with Hangar Marketing. While interning with them I was given many projects to work on, I was in charge of the build and design of various websites. It was such an amazing experience and I learned much from their guidance.

Along with web design I very much enjoy drawing. I use many different mediums, but the one I use the most right now is a program on my iPad called "Procreate".

I also enjoy trying new things and learning how to solve problems. Most of all I love my family and spending time with them.
